Our Story

This is "sound mixing" website. I want to find out the relation among various sound,including voice from human, voice from animals and the natural sound surrounding us. In order to remind people the definition of the music can be not limited with in classic, country, or Jazz, those kinds of music. On my website, users can mix sound randomly(click the action pictures). I hope people can understand every moment of our life is mysterious and beautiful.

Users can upload their voice and then mix natural or instrument sound together. Our team collected several voice from individuals, including B-box, beating desk, cappella, wind, breathing, dropping and even emergency alarming. All human voice are from Parsons DT students.
